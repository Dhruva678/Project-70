Achievements

Don Bosco High School is located in Kalyan, Thane. The children get the most out of their education as they are taught by a team of 5 professional and dedicated faculties in this State Board school.Don Bosco High School was launched in 2014. English is the primary medium of instruction for this school and the student teacher ratio is 25:1. The school takes pride of its excellent teaching methodology.The school provides education to students from class 9 to 10. 128 is approximately the current student strength of this school.The school has given exceptional results in the academic sphere and its students have excelled in extra co-curricular activities too.
